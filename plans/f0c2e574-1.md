## Current state

`HexPolyFp` sits at `libraries.yml` `done_through: 5` and is in Phase 6
(proof polishing). `PLAN/Phase6.md` calls for automation annotations:
"`@[grind]` for automated reasoning. Maximize what `simp` and `grind`
can close automatically."

The Phase 6 grind pass already landed for `HexPolyFp/Basic.lean`
(#7958). `HexPolyFp/Enumeration.lean` is the local gap: it is the
bounded-degree enumeration substrate behind the quotient-cardinality
arguments, and `rg '@\[grind|\bgrind\b' HexPolyFp/Enumeration.lean`
finds **no** `grind` annotations or in-file `grind` sites.

The module exposes exactly the equation- and proposition-shaped public
characterising facts downstream reasoning wants when rewriting over the
`coeffLists` / `polysBelowDegree` enumeration API:

- Equation-shaped (several already `@[simp]`): `coeffLists_zero`,
  `coeffLists_succ`, `coeffLists_length`, `polysBelowDegree_length`.
- Membership/iff facts: `length_of_mem_coeffLists`, `mem_coeffLists_iff`,
  `mem_polysBelowDegree_iff_degree_getD_lt`,
  `mem_polysBelowDegree_succ_iff_degree_getD_lt` (already `@[simp]`),
  `degree_getD_lt_of_mem_polysBelowDegree`,
  `mem_polysBelowDegree_of_degree_getD_lt`,
  `of_first_coeffs_eq_of_degree_getD_lt`.
- `nodup` facts: `coeffLists_nodup`, `polysBelowDegree_nodup`.

This does not overlap with the open Phase 6 grind passes for other
libraries' modules (`HexArith/ExtGcd.lean`, `HexMatrix/Basic.lean`); it
is the same polishing theme applied to a different `HexPolyFp` module.
