## Context

This audit is the natural follow-on to #5069's audit of the
`zpoly_dvd_trans` cluster. The two clusters are parallel:
`zpoly_dvd_trans` covers the `Hex.ZPoly`-level transitivity (whose
bespoke `Dvd` is the `Mul`-derived `semigroupDvd`), while
`fpPoly_dvd_trans` covers the `Hex.FpPoly p`-level transitivity
(whose bespoke `Dvd` is `instDvdOfAddOfMul`). Mathlib's
`dvd_trans` does not see through either bespoke instance, hence
the need for both helpers.
