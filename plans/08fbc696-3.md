## Current state

Euclidean (`xgcd`) Bezout-identity and gcd-divisibility cluster (lines
~2918-3040) is undocumented. These private theorems establish that
`xgcdAux` returns a Bezout combination of its inputs and that the returned
gcd both divides the inputs and is divisible by every common divisor. They
underpin the public `xgcd_bezout_of_divModLaws` wrapper (L2995, already
documented). These five private theorems carry no docstrings:

- `xgcdAux_gcd_eq_left_of_right_zero` (L2918)
- `xgcd_bezout_step` (L2928)
- `xgcdAux_bezout` (L2953)
- `xgcdAux_common_dvd_gcd` (L3006)
- `xgcdAux_gcd_dvd_inputs` (L3040)
