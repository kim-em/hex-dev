## Deliverables

Discharge `rabinTest_true_of_mathlib_checks` (the `sorry` at
`HexBerlekampMathlib/Basic.lean:532`), introducing only the reverse
transport helpers that leg needs:

1. **Positivity ⟹ `decide (0 < n)`.** From `0 < n` and `hdegree`,
   `decide (0 < basisSize f) = true`.

2. **Divisibility ⟹ `rabinDividesTest`.** From
   `toMathlibPolynomial f ∣ frobeniusPolynomial p n` derive
   `(frobeniusDiffMod f hmonic n).isZero = true`. The natural helper is
   the reverse of the forward leg's remainder lemma: Mathlib
   divisibility of the transported polynomial into `X^(p^n) - X` implies
   the executable remainder `frobeniusDiffMod f hmonic n` is zero, via
   `frobeniusXPowMod` / `modByMonic` transport and `toMathlibPolynomial_monic`.

3. **Coprimality ⟹ witnesses.** From, for each
   `d ∈ maximalProperDivisors n`,
   `IsCoprime (toMathlibPolynomial f) (frobeniusPolynomial p d)`, derive
   `(rabinWitnesses f hmonic).all Prod.snd = true`, i.e. each
   `isUnitPolynomial (DensePoly.gcd f (frobeniusDiffMod f hmonic d)) =
   true`. Move the Mathlib coprimality from `frobeniusPolynomial p d`
   to its reduction `frobeniusDiffMod f hmonic d` (executable value is
   `X^(p^d) - X mod f`), then transport `IsCoprime` back to a unit
   executable gcd (the reverse of the unit-gcd-to-coprime pattern, using
   `toMathlibPolynomial_gcd_associated`/`_normalize`).

**Premise check before opening a PR.** Confirm the reverse transport is
actually derivable:

- `frobeniusDiffMod`'s `modByMonic`-based definition must transport so
  that `toMathlibPolynomial f ∣ frobeniusPolynomial p k` forces the
  executable remainder to vanish. If the needed remainder-transport
  lemma (`toMathlibPolynomial (modByMonic ...) = ... % toMathlibPolynomial f`,
  or `(frobeniusDiffMod f hmonic k).isZero ↔ toMathlibPolynomial f ∣
  frobeniusPolynomial p k`) is absent, that helper is part of this
  deliverable.

  transports to a **unit** executable gcd without an extra hypothesis.
