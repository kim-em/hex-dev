## Current state

`HexPoly/Euclid.lean` lines ~4879–4922 contain five undocumented
private lemmas about the integer-coefficient gcd fold used to compute
polynomial content (`Nat.gcd`-over-`natAbs` folds and their interaction
with `trimTrailingZerosList`). They are part of the Phase 6
docstring-coverage pass (`PLAN/Phase6.md`: "docstring coverage meets
the rule in `SPEC/design-principles.md`"). The declarations:

- `foldl_gcd_natAbs_mul_const_int` — scaling every coefficient by `c`
  pulls `c.natAbs` out of the gcd fold.
- `foldl_gcd_natAbs_of_all_zero` — a gcd fold over all-zero coefficients
  returns the accumulator unchanged.
- `trimTrailingZerosList_cons_int` — the defining `cons` unfolding of
  `trimTrailingZerosList` on `Int` lists.
- `all_zero_of_trimTrailingZerosList_nil` — if trimming trailing zeros
  empties the list, every entry was zero.
- `foldl_gcd_natAbs_trim_eq` — trimming trailing zeros before the gcd
  fold gives the same result as folding the untrimmed list.
