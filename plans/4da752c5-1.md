the entire `factorWithBound f B` output comes from the

guarantees the `squareFreeCore` is primitive (positive content

recorded factor entries therefore come entirely from the
normalization prefix (`xPower` and `repeatedPart`) plus possibly
the constant core itself if it is not filtered out by
`shouldRecordPolynomialFactor`.

For an HO-1 capstone arm, the per-entry irreducibility obligation
on this branch reduces to:

  rule them out from the recorded entry set);
