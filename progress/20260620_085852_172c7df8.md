# HexPolyFp Phase 7 reference chapter (#8221)

## Accomplished

Authored `HexManual/Chapters/HexPolyFp.lean`, the Phase 7 reference
chapter for the computational-layer library `HexPolyFp`, modelled on
the sibling computational chapter `HexPoly.lean` and the worked-`#guard`
pattern from `HexGFqField.lean`:

- Introduction framing `HexPolyFp` as the prime-field specialization of
  `HexPoly` (`FpPoly p` is an `abbrev` over `DensePoly (ZMod64 p)`).
- Core type section: `{docstring Hex.FpPoly}` and
  `{docstring Hex.FpPoly.Irreducible}`.
- Principal operations: `powModMonic`, `composeModMonic`,
  `frobeniusXMod`, `frobeniusXPowMod`, the `SquareFreeFactor` /
  `SquareFreeDecomposition` records, `squareFreeDecomposition`, and
  `weightedProduct`, all via `{docstring}`.
- A quotient-by-modulus section documenting `Hex.FpPoly.Quotient` and
  stating explicitly that the field surface parametrizes over
  `FpPoly.Irreducible g` as a hypothesis (no unconditional `Field`
  instance), per the issue.
- A worked `#guard` block over `FpPoly 5` with values copied verbatim
  from the passing `HexPolyFp/Conformance.lean` guards (powModMonic,
  Frobenius, composition, weighted product, square-free decomposition +
  reconstruction).
- Key correctness theorems: `composeModMonic_eq_mod`,
  `frobeniusXPowMod_mod_eq_monomial_mod`, the three
  `squareFreeDecomposition_*` laws, and the irreducibility-gated
  `Quotient.mul_inv_cancel` / `inv_mul_cancel`.
- Cross-references to `{ref "hex-poly"}` and `{ref "hex-mod-arith"}`
  (both resolve) and forward to `HexGFqField`.

Wired the chapter into `HexManual.lean` (import + `{include 0 ...}`) and
bumped `libraries.yml[HexPolyFp].done_through: 6 → 7`.
