## Accomplished

Issue #7532: added `exists_dvd_core_of_dvd_toMonic`
(`HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/Basic.lean`), the inverse monic-correspondent
existence lemma — one of the two bridge lemmas gating #7362's `cover` field. It
descends a monic irreducible factor `g` of `M := (toMonic core).monic` back to an
**original** primitive integer factor `f := primitivePart (dilate (leadingCoeff
core) g)` of `core`, recovering all four conjuncts the assembly needs:
irreducibility of `toPolynomial f`, `f ∣ core`, `normalizeFactorSign f = f`, and
the defining `primitivePart (dilate lc g) = f`. It is the converse of #7455's
`exists_monicCorrespondent_of_dvd` (forward map only).

Proof structure:
- **`f ∣ core`** is read off the keystone `dilate lc M = scale (lc^(d-1)) core`
  (`dilate_monic_toMonic`). Writing `g ∣ M` as `M = g * cof` with `cof` monic
  (leading-coeff multiplicativity) and dilating by `lc`,
  `scale (lc^(d-1)) core = dilate lc g * dilate lc cof
   = scale cg f * scale ccof pcof = scale (cg·ccof) (f·pcof)` over the primitive
  parts `f`, `pcof` of the two dilations. Taking `primitivePart` of both sides —
  via a local helper `primitivePart (scale s p) = p` for `0 < s`, `Primitive p`,
  and Gauss (`primitive_mul`, so `f·pcof` is primitive) — collapses the positive
  content scalars and yields `core = f * pcof`, hence `f ∣ core`.
- **Irreducibility** transfers through a new `iff` helper
  `irreducible_toPolynomial_dilate_iff`, factored out of the existing forward
  `irreducible_toPolynomial_monicCorrespondent` (which now calls `.mp` on it; the
  inverse uses `.mpr`). The body is the unchanged automorphism/Gauss argument,
  restated as an `Iff` (both Gauss rewrites on the goal, ending `exact hiff`).
- **`normalizeFactorSign f = f`** from `0 < leadingCoeff f`: the dilation's top
  coefficient is `lc^(g.size-1) > 0` (via `size_dilate_eq_of_monic_of_ne_zero`),
  and stripping the positive content `cg` keeps the sign positive.

No new `axiom`/`sorry`/`native_decide`; no executable change.
