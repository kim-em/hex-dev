## Current frontier

- The corrected `factor_unique` adds two hypotheses beyond the original
  shape from #4171: `normalizeFactorSign` and nonconstant per entry on
  both sides, plus `Hex.Factorization.product φ ≠ 0`. The nonzero
  hypothesis closes the original-statement counterexample where
  `scalar = 0` produces a zero product for any factor list.
- `factor_unique_of_product` requires the caller to supply matching
  `normalizeFactorSign` and nonconstant evidence for `Hex.factor f`. These
  will be discharged by the executable-factor well-formedness lemmas being
  built under the #4170 chain.
