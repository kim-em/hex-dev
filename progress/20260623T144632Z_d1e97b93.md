# #8327 subset Hensel uniqueness core

## Accomplished

Added `henselLiftData_liftedSubset_congr_of_modP` in
`HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/Basic.lean`.

The theorem packages the subset/complement product congruence, the mod-`p`
subset representation, complement cancellation, subset/complement coprimality,
degree equality, and `HexHenselMathlib.hensel_unique` into an additive
conclusion:

`liftedFactorProduct (henselLiftData target B primeData) (lifted S) ≡ factor
(mod primeData.p ^ B)`.

It generalizes the proof core of `henselLiftData_represents_lifted_of_modP` by
requiring only a competing modular product congruence
`factor * cofactor ≡ target`, rather than an integer equality.  This is the
piece needed for M1-style `monicTarget` coordinates where the target comparison
is modular.

Verified with `lake build HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib` green.
