# 2026-05-09 (UTC) — issue #2786 (HexPolyFp DDF saturation lemma)

## Accomplished

- Added a generic `Lean.Grind.CommRing`-parameterised Leibniz rule
  `Hex.DensePoly.derivative_mul` in `HexPoly/Euclid.lean` together
  with its supporting helpers (`fold_add_range_succ_commring`,
  `weighted_diagonal_fold_aux_commring`, `weighted_diagonal_fold_commring`,
  `coeff_derivative_commring`, `mulCoeffSum_derivative_product_rule`).
  The proofs follow the existing `rat_*` skeleton but use
  `Lean.Grind.Semiring.natCast_succ`/`natCast_one`/`natCast_zero`
  in place of `Rat`-specific `simp` rewrites. `Lean.Grind.Semiring.natCast`
  is registered as an `instance 1100` to override the standalone
  `NatCast (ZMod64 p)` instance, ensuring `derivative_mul` unifies at
  call-sites.
- Added the strong square-free characterization
  `Hex.Berlekamp.isUnitPolynomial_of_squareFree_of_squared_dvd` in
  `HexBerlekamp/RabinSoundness.lean`: from `gcd r r' = 1` and
  `g * g ∣ r`, `isUnitPolynomial g = true`. Proved via Leibniz
  on `r = g · g · h` to extract `g ∣ r'`, then `g ∣ gcd(r, r') = 1`.
- Added the saturation lemma
  `Hex.Berlekamp.isUnitPolynomial_gcd_quotient_of_squareFree`:
  for square-free `r`, the new residual `r / gcd(r, d)` is coprime
  to `d`. This is the bridge from DDF buckets to the Frobenius-
  divisibility step needed by `distinctDegreeFactor_bucket_invariants`.
- Helper lemmas: `fp_eq_mul_div_of_dvd`, `fp_swap_inner_mul`,
  `fp_swap_left_mul` package the divisibility arithmetic so the
  saturation proof is one trans-chain.
