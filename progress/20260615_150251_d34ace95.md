- `dvd_of_comp_unit_mul_X {R} [CommRing R] {u} (hu : IsUnit u) {a b}`:

Proof route, as the issue's sketch anticipated: the substitution `X ↦ u·X` is a
ring automorphism with inverse `X ↦ u⁻¹·X`. Concretely,
`(C u * X).comp (C u⁻¹ * X) = X` (via `mul_comp`/`C_comp`/`X_comp`/`C_mul` and
`u·u⁻¹ = 1`), so by `comp_assoc` + `comp_X` the inverse substitution undoes the
forward one. Applying `Polynomial.compRingHom (C u⁻¹ * X)` to the hypothesis via
`map_dvd` and rewriting with `coe_compRingHom_apply` reflects the divisibility.

Used `Polynomial.compRingHom` (the bundled comp ring hom) rather than building an
`eval₂RingHom` by hand — it is exactly `fun p => p.comp q` and gives `map_dvd`
directly. Not a one-line Mathlib cite: no existing lemma states comp-by-a-unit
reflects divisibility (the #7474 worker's failed inline attempt was accurate).
