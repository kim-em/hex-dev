    - Module-level `IO.Ref` cache `flintDriverRef`.

## Caveat documented in driver / Lean docstring

`lean-bench`'s `setup_fixed_benchmark` shape spawns one fresh
`hexfoo_bench` child process per measured repeat, which means the
`flintDriverRef` cache is per-process — each repeat pays one
python-flint startup. Within a single child the driver still
amortises across every measured call (no per-call
`IO.Process.output`, no per-call `IO.FS.writeFile`), but for
`fixed_benchmark` consumers the per-call wall time will be
dominated by python-flint startup at small ladder rungs. The
operative number for HO-18/HO-21..HO-26's overhead-adjusted ratios
is the steady-state per-call protocol overhead inside one child;
each consuming HO measures and records that figure in its headline
report.
