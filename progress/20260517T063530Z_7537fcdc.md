### Decisions

* The new classifier helper (2) was introduced because the existing
  3-way classifier exposes the normalization repeated-part fallback,
  which under `hcomplete` is not directly disjoint from
  `xPower ∪ coreFactors` and so cannot be ruled out by simple set
  intersection. The complete-expansion classifier folds the
  repeated-part case into the irreducibility track via
  `_mem_xPower_or_core_of_expansionComplete`, leaving only a clean
  two-way irreducible-or-residual disjunction for the umbrella.

* The residual sub-case was sub-issued rather than discharged inline.
  The proof requires bridging
  `splitIntegerRootFactorsAux_product` (split residual times
  polyProduct equals core) through `HexPolyZMathlib.toPolynomial_mul`
  and `Polynomial.isPrimitive_of_dvd` to get a primitive residual,
  then a Gauss-style argument that a primitive degree-1 polynomial
  over `ℤ[X]` is irreducible (degree-0 case is ruled out by the
  recorded-entry filter on `±1`). The helper is independent of the
  slow-vs-fast dispatch and will be shared with the fast-path
  quadratic arm umbrella #4571; landing it as a separate sub-issue
  (now #4579) is the cleanest path.

* `#4571` (the fast-path quadratic arm) was not yet pushed when this
  issue was claimed; the slow-path umbrella restates the structural
  pattern inline rather than waiting on it, and #4579 carries the
  shared residual obligation that both arms will consume.
