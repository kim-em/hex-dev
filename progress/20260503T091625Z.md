  Verified `pod config` parses and `pod status` runs cleanly.
- pod ships 70 passing tests including 32 new ones in
  `tests/test_auto_backend.py` covering selection logic, per-backend
  quota checks, isolated-config dir resolution, and instruction-file
  warnings under auto mode.

  any unexpected behaviour around per-iteration backend switching that
  the unit tests didn't catch.
