  `normalizeFactorSign g = g` from `Polynomial.IsPrimitive gPoly` and

Key proof technique changes vs. the unscaled primitive variant
(`mem_T_iff_exists_irreducibleFactor_representingSubset_of_primitive_pos_lc_core`,
line 9017):

* `hcand_monic.normalize_eq_self` replaced by
  `normalize_toPolynomial_of_normalizeFactorSign_id` applied to the
  candidate's `normalizeFactorSign` fixed-point property.
* `Polynomial.natDegree_multiset_prod_of_monic _ hnf_monic` replaced
  by `Polynomial.natDegree_multiset_prod _
    (UniqueFactorizationMonoid.zero_notMem_normalizedFactors _)`
  (works on `Polynomial ℤ` since every normalized factor is nonzero).
* Per-factor packaging routed through the new scaled variant; the
  unscaled `recombinationCandidate` is never identified with the
  scaled candidate.

For `coverAtMin_*`, the `representingSubset_subset_of_dvd_scaledRecombinationCandidate_of_primitive_pos_lc_core`
