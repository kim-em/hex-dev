- `BHKS.correctionWeighted_sq_le_of_coeff_sq_le`
  (`HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/BadVectorAuxiliary.lean`): from a
  coordinate square bound `c_j ^ 2 ≤ D`, the weighted correction
  `c_j ^ 2 · p ^ (2 (k − ℓ_j))` is at most the uniform `D · p ^ (2k)`,
  using `p ^ (2 (k − ℓ_j)) ≤ p ^ (2k)` (since `k − ℓ_j ≤ k`, `p ≥ 1`). This
  is the `hcorrectionWeightedBound` content with the canonical value
  `correctionWeightedBound := D · p ^ (2k)`.
