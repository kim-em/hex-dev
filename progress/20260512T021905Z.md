Closed the comparator-coverage loophole in the Phase-4 doctrine. Of the 14 libraries currently at `done_through ≥ 4`, only HexLLL had any external comparator wired; the other 13 silently declared none. The existing SPEC §"External comparators" wording permitted that silence — per-library SPEC authors decided what was "architecturally important" with no obligation to record the decision.
