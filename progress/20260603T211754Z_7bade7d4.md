## Current frontier

The real-l2-norm coefficient-power sub-bound
`‖core‖₂^aux.natDegree ≤ bhksPaperCoeffNormFactorReal core` is now a
one-line discharge for any caller that has the BHKS auxiliary polynomial
in hand and can supply `core ≠ 0`. This closes the analytic step itself.

The factored cap-separation chain (`bhksPaperThresholdReal_chain_lt_p_pow_kLocalFactorDegree_factored`
in `HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/Recovery.lean`) still asks for the
integer-arithmetic shape
`(coeffL2NormBound core : ℝ) ^ aux.natDegree ≤ bhksPaperCoeffNormFactorReal core`,
where `coeffL2NormBound core = ⌈√(coeffNormSq core)⌉ ≥ ‖core‖₂`.  The
real-l2-norm composite proved here is in the natural BHKS direction
(`‖core‖₂` rather than the integer ceiling), so a caller routing through
the factored chain still has to bridge from `coeffL2NormBound` down to
`‖core‖₂` somewhere — that integer-vs-real conversion is the remaining
piece of the coefficient-power discharge.
