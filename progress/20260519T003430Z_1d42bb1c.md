- Diagnosis: instance diamond between
  `Lean.Grind.Semiring.toAdd (ZMod64 p) _` (the `Add S` projection
  used implicitly in the wrapper's LHS, because the wrapper's only
  `Add` witness comes through `[Lean.Grind.Semiring S]`) and the
  direct `instance : Add (ZMod64 p)` from `HexPolyFp/Basic.lean`.
  The two are propositionally and definitionally equal — `exact`
  succeeds, and `@Lean.Grind.Semiring.toAdd (ZMod64 p) _ = (inferInstance : Add (ZMod64 p)) := rfl`
  type-checks — but `rw`'s higher-order syntactic matching does not
  see them as the same, and `simp` apparently does not unfold the
  `toAdd` projection either.
