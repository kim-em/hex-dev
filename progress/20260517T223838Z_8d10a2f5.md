# Decompose #4844 (adjacent-swap bordered-minor identities)

## Accomplished

Claimed #4844 (`HexGramSchmidt: prove adjacent-swap bordered-minor identities
for scaled coefficients`) and audited the available Mathlib-free determinant
infrastructure against the requested deliverable.

The audit found that `HexMatrix/Determinant.lean` exposes only the SCALED
Desnanot-Jacobi identity `Hex.Matrix.det_desnanot_jacobi_mul`:

```
det M * (A * B - C * D) = det M * (det M * Inner)
```

(over a `Lean.Grind.CommRing`). The corresponding unscaled form `A * B - C * D
= det M * Inner` lives only on the Mathlib side
(`HexMatrixMathlib/Determinant/Core.lean`'s `desnanot_jacobi_borderedMinor`),
which uses Mathlib permutation lemmas and integral-domain cancellation.

Combined with the row/column permutation that must be tracked to identify
`scaledCoeffMatrix (Matrix.rowSwap b km1 k) i km1 _` with the appropriate
single-deletion minor of a permuted form of `scaledCoeffMatrix b i k`, plus
the boundary case where the multiplier `nu[i][k]` is zero, the
above-prev and above-curr identities each fan out into a non-trivial
multi-step proof. The full task is well past one session of Lean work.

Decomposed into three follow-ups, with depend-on edges populated:

- **#4852** — Mathlib-free `Int`-valued unscaled Desnanot-Jacobi
  (`det M ≠ 0 ⇒ A*B - C*D = det M * Inner`). Direct cancellation of
  `det_desnanot_jacobi_mul`.
- **#4853** — above-prev bordered-minor identity (depends on #4852).
- **#4854** — above-curr bordered-minor identity (depends on #4852 and
  #4853, since one natural derivation combines above-prev with the
  gram-det adjacent-swap relation).

Posted the decomposition breadcrumb on #4844 and ran
`coordination skip 4844 ...` so the planner can close it once the
sub-issues fully cover the parent.
