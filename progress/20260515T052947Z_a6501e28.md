## Current frontier

The five size-reduce bridge-bound theorems
(`gramDet_sizeReduce`, `scaledCoeffs_sizeReduce_pivot/lower/other_row/
above_pivot`) named in #4151 remain in `HexGramSchmidt/Update.lean` with
a docstring block explaining why they stay. They are referenced from
`LLLState.sizeReduceColumn` in `HexLLL/Basic.lean` at five sites (L296,
L323, L349, L363, L369) to discharge the `ν_eq` and `d_eq` structure
fields when constructing the size-reduced state. Moving them to the
bridge without first refactoring those discharges would:

- Cascade `sorry` into `LLLState.sizeReduceColumn`'s field discharges,
  which would in turn make `(s.sizeReduce k).ν_eq` and
  `(s.sizeReduce k).d_eq` sorry-derived. `sizeReduce_independent_core`
  in `HexLLL/Basic.lean` consumes `(s.sizeReduce k).d_eq` and so would
  become sorry-derived, as would the public
  `LLLState.sizeReduce_independent` wrapper that Round 1 placed in
  `HexLLLMathlib/Independent.lean`. The downstream LLL termination /
  short-vector chain depends on this, so the cascade is non-trivial.

- Alternatively, import `HexGramSchmidtMathlib.Update` from
  `HexLLL/Basic.lean` and add `HexGramSchmidtMathlib` to `HexLLL`'s
  `deps` in `libraries.yml`. This is forbidden by the directive
  (#3899): "No Mathlib-free file may import a bridge module
  mid-relocation. The top-down ordering above achieves this." Even
  though the new bridge module is Mathlib-free in import surface (only
  imports `HexGramSchmidt.Update`), the boundary discipline is
  categorical.

Issue #3916 ("HO-43 prerequisite: split LLLState ν invariant to
executable scaledCoeffs") is the natural home for the
`sizeReduceColumn` refactor that removes the bridge-bound calls; it is
currently blocked on `#3899` but is the unblocking step for the
remaining five size-reduce theorems.
