## Premise correction

The issue body says "Mathlib-free" and points placement at
`HexLLL/Basic.lean (Mathlib-free), reusing HexGramSchmidt/Int.lean.”
That is right for the `Bool` def but not for the soundness theorem: the
required bridges (`scaledCoeffs_eq`, `basis_normSq`, `gramDet_pos`,
`StepWitness.ofGram`) all live in `HexGramSchmidtMathlib/Int.lean`, so
the soundness theorem necessarily lives on the Mathlib side. This
mirrors the existing split for `Hex.LLLState.ofBasis_valid` and
`Hex.HexLLLMathlib.lovasz_check_iff_isLLLReduced_pair`.
