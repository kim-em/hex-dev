### Executable congruence lemmas (`HexHensel/Linear.lean`)

Each linear Hensel step adjusts a factor only by
`LinearLiftResult.liftScaledIncrement p k r`, which is `≡ 0 (mod p^k)`
(`congr_liftScaledIncrement_zero`); for `1 ≤ k` that descends to `mod p`. So
the stepped/looped/lifted factors stay congruent to their inputs modulo the
base prime. Added, mirroring the quadratic structure:

- `congr_reduceModPow_add_increment_mod_base` (private helper)
- `linearHenselStep_g_congr_mod_base`, `linearHenselStep_h_congr_mod_base`
- `henselLiftLoop_g_congr_mod_base`, `henselLiftLoop_h_congr_mod_base` (private,
  loop induction)
- `henselLift_g_congr_mod_base`, `henselLift_h_congr_mod_base` (public; the
  linear-loop analogues of `henselLiftQuadratic_g/h_congr_mod_base`).

These need only `1 ≤ k` and `[ZMod64.Bounds p]` — no product invariant, since
the increment congruence is purely structural.
