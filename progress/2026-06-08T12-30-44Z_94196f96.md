# LLL capstones (#6590)

## Accomplished

Closed the loop-invariant induction tying together #6587, #6588, #6589, and
delivered the unconditional public LLL capstones called for by #6590.

In `HexLLL/Basic.lean`:
- `Matrix.rowAdd_memLattice_iff`: row-add (with distinct rows) preserves the
  integer row lattice.
- `LLLState.sizeReduceColumn_memLattice_iff` and
  `LLLState.sizeReduce_memLattice_iff`: per-column and full size reduction
  preserve the lattice.
- `lllLoop_memLattice_iff`: fuel-bounded loop preserves the lattice.

In `HexLLLMathlib/Independent.lean`:
- `LLLState.sizeReduce_coeffs_row_of_ne`: rows `i ≠ k` of the Gram-Schmidt
  coefficient matrix survive `sizeReduce k` intact (the basis-row update
  touches only row `k`).
- `LLLState.sizeReduce_prefixLLLReduced`: the prefix invariant at level `k`
  is preserved by `sizeReduce k`.
- `prefixLLLReduced.advance`: extend the prefix by one row given new
  size-reducedness for row `k` and the rational Lovász condition at
  `(k - 1, k)`.
- `prefixLLLReduced_one`: vacuous initial invariant.
- `prefixLLLReduced_to_isLLLReduced`: the prefix invariant at `k = n` is
  exactly `isLLLReduced`.
- `LLLState.lllLoop_isLLLReduced_of_fuel_gt_measure`: invariant induction
  over loop fuel.
- `LLLState.lllLoop_independent`: independence preservation through the
  loop (parallel mini-induction).
- `Hex.lll_isLLLReduced`: unconditional LLL reducedness of the public
  entry point.
- `Hex.lll_memLattice_iff`: `Hex.lll` preserves the integer lattice.
- `Hex.lll_first_row_norm_sq_le_unconditional`: unconditional integer
  short-vector bound.
- `HexLLLMathlib.lll_mem_latticeSubmodule_iff` and
  `HexLLLMathlib.lll_first_row_norm_sq_le_unconditional`: Mathlib
  `Submodule`-flavoured analogues, threaded through
  `reduced_first_row_norm_sq_le_of_mem_latticeSubmodule`.

`HexLLLMathlib.Independent` now imports `HexLLLMathlib.Basic` so the loop
induction can call `lovasz_check_iff_isLLLReduced_pair`.
