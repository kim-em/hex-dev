structure's `B` parameter, but the structure's `B` is the precision
*count* passed to `henselLiftData`, and the #4537 slow-path wrapper
takes the structure at
`B := precisionForCoeffBound (defaultFactorCoeffBound f) primeData.p`
(the precision actually consumed by `exhaustiveCoreFactorsWithBound` in
the public `Hex.factor f` entry path).  The parametric constructor (2)
covers both: instantiate at `defaultFactorCoeffBound f` for the literal
issue shape, or at `precisionForCoeffBound (…) primeData.p` (via the
outer-bound specialisation (3)) for the HO-1 path that actually
unblocks #4170.
