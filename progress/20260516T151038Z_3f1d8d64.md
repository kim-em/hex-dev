## Accomplished

- Added private theorem `subsetSplits_prefix_exists_bit_diff_aux` in
  `HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/Basic.lean` (≈325 lines including
  docstring). This is the mask-level structural enumeration-order content
  of `Hex.subsetSplits` on a `Nodup` input: given the `mask_S`-split at
  the boundary `pre ++ _ :: suffix` of `Hex.subsetSplits xs`, with the
  `mask_T`-split sitting somewhere inside `pre`, the helper produces a
  position `i'` where `mask_T[i']` is `false` and `mask_S[i']` is `true`.

- The proof is direct induction on `xs`, with the `Nodup` precondition
  ruling out the duplicate-element ambiguity that the issue's spec
  flagged. Cases on `(mask_S.head, mask_T.head)`:
  - `(true, false)` immediate at `i = 0`.
  - `(false, true)` impossible: split shape rules out the natural
    L_false/L_true partition under `x ∉ xs'`.
  - `(true, true)` / `(false, false)` recurse on the tail after lifting
    the L_true / L_false structure equation back to `subsetSplits xs'`
    via `List.map_eq_append_iff`.

- All four cases compile with no `sorry` / `axiom` / `native_decide` /
  `TODO` / `FIXME`. Full file builds clean
  (`lake build HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib.Basic` /
  `lake build HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib`).
