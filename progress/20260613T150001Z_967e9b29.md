# Progress: #6837 — raw Yun tail provider packaging

## Accomplished

- Claimed #6837 and worked in `HexPolyFp/SquareFree.lean`.
- Added `YunDerivativeActiveRawStateProvider`, a single raw-state provider
  carrying the raw `c` and `w` reachability/nonzero facts needed by the
  executable Yun recursion.
- Added adapters from that provider to the existing
  `YunDerivativeActiveLevelStateProvider` and
  `YunDerivativeActiveRawTailProvider`.
- Refactored the weighted-product proof chain
  (`squareFreeAuxRevContribution_derivative_active_pow_obligation`,
  `squareFreeAuxRevContribution_correct_pow_of_nonzero`,
  `squareFreeAuxRevContribution_correct`,
  `squareFreeAux_weightedProduct_nonzero`, and
  `squareFree_weightedProduct`) to take the single raw-state provider and
  derive the older providers internally.

## Current frontier

The executable SquareFree definitions are unchanged. The normalized product
bridge remains in use, and the proof chain no longer threads separate level
and raw-tail provider arguments through public/private weighted-product
wrappers.

## Next step

Close #6837 through the PR for this change. A future issue can try to close
the raw-state provider itself, but it must account for the existing raw
`DensePoly.gcd` scalar behavior; normalized state alone is not enough for the
raw executable tail obligations.
