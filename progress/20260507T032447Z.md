## Current frontier

- The key design question is not whether claim-time collaborator validation is
  useful, but whether it is being placed at the right boundary: it covers worker
  issue execution, but not planner reads of existing issue bodies unless similar
  gating or filtering is added earlier in the queue/list path.
- Another unsettled point is provenance policy for edited issue bodies and
  comments: whether trust should attach to original authorship, current content
  editors, or a strict "all contributing authors must be trusted" rule.
