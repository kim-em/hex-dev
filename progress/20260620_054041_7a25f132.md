# HexModArith Phase 7 reference chapter (#8197)

## Accomplished

Authored the `HexManual` reference chapter for `HexModArith` and bumped
the library to Phase 7.

- **New chapter** `HexManual/Chapters/HexModArith.lean`: a Verso chapter
  modelled on `HexManual/Chapters/HexPoly.lean`. Covers the `ZMod64`
  residue type and `ZMod64.Bounds` typeclass; constructors/conversions
  (`ofNat`, `toNat`, the representativity bridge `toNat_ofNat`); the ring
  operations (`add`/`sub`/`neg`/`mul`/`pow`/`inv`) with a compiling
  `ZMod64 7` worked example; the Barrett/Montgomery hot-loop contexts
  (`BarrettCtx`, `MontCtx`, `MontResidue`, with `mulMod_eq_mul` and
  `fromMont_mulMont_toMont` as the agreement theorems) with a second
  compiling worked example; the key correctness theorems
  (`toNat_*` representativity laws, `inv_mul_eq_one_of_coprime`, and the
  prime-modulus field facts `eq_zero_or_eq_zero_of_mul_eq_zero`,
  `inv_mul_eq_one_of_prime`, `pow_prime`); and cross-references to the
  `HexArith` dependency and the existing `HexModArithMathlib` bridge
  chapter (`{ref "hex-mod-arith-mathlib"}` resolves).
- **Wired into the aggregator**: added the import and `{include 0 ...}`
  to `HexManual.lean`.
- **Phase 7 bump**: `libraries.yml[HexModArith].done_through: 6 → 7`.
- **Supporting doc fix**: added field docstrings to `ZMod64.Bounds`
  (`pPos`, `pLeR`) and `ZMod64` (`val`, `isLt`) in
  `HexModArith/Basic.lean`. The `{docstring}` directive renders struct
  fields and errors on undocumented ones, so these were required for the
  chapter to build; they are a genuine doc improvement.
