### BZMathlib A2 abstract-bound `_of_bound` propagation (9 PRs)

Every A2 recovery / precision lemma is being replicated with an abstract
bound `B' : Nat`, `hvalid : ∀ i, (factor.coeff i).natAbs ≤ B'`, and
`2 * B' < d.p ^ d.k` in place of the core-shape
`defaultFactorCoeffBound core` constraint. The originals are rewired as
thin wrappers that instantiate `B' := defaultFactorCoeffBound core` and

precision side conditions from the executable core shape so downstream
