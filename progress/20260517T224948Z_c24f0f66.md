  (previously file-`private`); supplies the `hnorm` precondition of

  bridges (`squareFreeCore_leadingCoeff_pos_of_ne_zero`,

* `Hex.expandRepeatedPartFactorArray_residual_eq_one_of_factorPower_decomposition_of_pos_lc`
  (#4778) for the non-monic public expansion-complete surface (required

Per-factor `0 < leadingCoeff q` follows from
`normalizeFactorSign q = q` (otherwise `scale (-1) q = q` forces all
coefficients to zero, contradicting irreducibility-derived `q ≠ 0`)
combined with `leadingCoeff_ne_zero_of_ne_zero`. Per-factor
`0 < q.degree?.getD 0` is immediate from `q.size = 2`.

The fuel bound `qe.2 + 1 ≤ rp.size + 1` is discharged internally: each
`factorPower qe.1 qe.2` divides the decomposition foldl product (=
`repeatedPart`); the `dvd_foldl_one_of_mem` inline helper (mirrors the
proof shape of `linearFactor_dvd_listFoldl_of_mem` for arbitrary
ZPoly elements) supplies the divisibility, and an inline induction
on `e` gives `e + 1 ≤ (factorPower q e).size` for any `q.size ≥ 2`,
mirroring the `hfactorPower_size_ge` argument in
`reassemblyExpansionComplete_singleton_of_irreducible`.

The pairwise non-association lemma
`quadraticIntegerRootFactors?_pairwise_not_associated` deliverable
(#4604 deliverable 2) was already landed by #4785 (commit `c2bebd78`).
The discharger does not consume it directly: the no-tail-divisibility
chain through `factorPower_cover_not_dvd_tail_of_irreducible_squarefree`
plus the per-factor `Nodup` derivation already inside #4807 covers the
non-association obligation as required by the expansion helper.
