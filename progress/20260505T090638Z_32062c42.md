## Accomplished

Claimed #2294 and proved
`GF2Poly.dvd_xPowSubX_iff_frobeniusDiffMod_isZero` in
`HexGF2/RabinSoundness.lean`. The leaf bridges the absolute polynomial
`X^(2^k) + X` (char 2 = `X^(2^k) − X`) to the packed Frobenius
remainder `xpow2kMod f k + (X mod f)`.

Strategy: avoid the Berlekamp proof's full `(p*q) % f = ((p%f)*(q%f)) %
f` infrastructure by working at the divisibility level.

- **Char-2 freshman's dream** `(a + b) * (a + b) = a*a + b*b` — three
  distributions plus an XOR cancellation of the two cross terms.
- **`mod_add_self_dvd`** `f ∣ ((divMod p f).2 + p)` — direct from
  `divMod_spec` using `add_self` to cancel the `p % f` term.
- **`dvd_xpow2kMod_add_monomial`** `f ∣ (xpow2kMod f k + monomial(2^k))`
  by induction on `k`. The successor step squares the IH using the
  freshman lemma (which lifts `f ∣ (a+b)` to `f ∣ a² + b²` via
  `dvd_mul_left'`), then adds another `mod_add_self_dvd` to absorb the
  un-reduced square into its `% f` reduction.
- **`dvd_xPowSubX_add_frobeniusDiffMod`** sums the previous lemma at
  `monomial 1` and at `monomial(2^k)` and rearranges via an `ext_coeff`
  truth-table identity.

Once the absolute-modular sum is divisible, the iff falls out by case
on `f`:

- `f = 0`: `0 ∣ x → x = 0`, immediate.
- `f ≠ 0`: `xpow2kMod f k` and `monomial 1 % f` are reduced (both are
  `% f` outputs), their sum is reduced (`add_reduced_of_reduced`), and
  a reduced divisor must be zero (`reduced_dvd_eq_zero`).

The reduced helpers (`coeff_eq_false_of_reduced_le`,
`add_reduced_of_reduced`, `reduced_dvd_eq_zero`) mirror the private
versions in `HexGF2/Euclid.lean`; they are inlined here as private
helpers rather than promoted, since they aren't used elsewhere yet.

The pre-existing private helpers `dvd_refl'`, `dvd_zero'`, `dvd_add'`,
`dvd_mul_left'`, `mul_dvd_mul_left'`, `mulXk_zero'`, `one_mul'`,
`mul_one'` were relocated to before the now-proven theorem so the proof
can use them; the geometric-series lemma `monomial_add_one_dvd_geom`
later in the file still resolves them as before.
