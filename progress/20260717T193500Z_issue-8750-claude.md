      the 0.15 boundary; `n³` is a strictly worse fit everywhere, so the
      report's "reach n³" option is not data-supported.
    - Bounded-height witness family (seeded, height ≤10) widens the sub-word
      band to `n≈56` (vs Wilkinson going multiword by `n≈24`) but still fails:
      log-x range ~1.25 → range check, residual `cMax/cMin≈1.73` > 1.5.
  - **Concern 5 (refineTo t²)** is a schoolbook-vs-GMP model mismatch: at the
    degree-3 fixture the cost is O(1) t-bit GMP ops, sub-quadratic in every
    reachable band (~t^1.1 allocation, ~t^1.58 Karatsuba), never t². Higher
    fixture degree scales the constant, not the t-exponent.
  - **Concerns 1 & 2 (isolate non-monotonicity; compare narrow range)** are
    the genuinely benchmark-design items (smooth family / widen), but a clean
    n⁵ slope fit needs a quiet box; not certified under contention.
