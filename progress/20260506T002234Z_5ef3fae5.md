In `HexPolyFp/Quotient.lean`:

- Added private helpers `zmod64_one_ne_zero` and `fpPoly_one_ne_zero`,
  and the public `Quotient.one_ne_zero` (quotient nontriviality under a
  positive-degree modulus).
- Added `listProd : List (Quotient g _ _) → Quotient g _ _` as a
  right-fold over `(· * ·)` with unit `1`, plus `listProd_nil`,
  `listProd_cons`, and `listProd_append`.
- Added `listProd_perm`: list-product invariance under `List.Perm`,
  proved by induction on `Perm` using `mul_assoc`/`mul_comm`.
- Added `listProd_map_mul_left`:
  `listProd (xs.map (a * ·)) = a^xs.length * listProd xs`.
- Added `listProd_ne_zero`: under monic irreducible positive-degree `g`,
  the product of a list of nonzero quotient elements is nonzero.
- Added the deliverable `pow_pred_card_eq_one_of_ne_zero`: for nonzero
  `a` modulo monic irreducible positive-degree `g`,
  `a ^ (p ^ g.degree?.getD 0 - 1) = 1`. Proof composes the
  `nonzeroElements_map_mul_left_perm` permutation theorem (from #2417)
  with the new product-factorisation and product-nonzero results, then
  cancels the common product by multiplying through by its inverse.
