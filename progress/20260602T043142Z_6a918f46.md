    `(leadingCoeff f)⁻¹ * f.coeff (n * p)` once the leadingCoeff identity
    is used to align the inverses. Doc-comment explicitly notes this is
    a computation-level identity, *not* the forbidden normalized-to-raw
    reachability bridge.
