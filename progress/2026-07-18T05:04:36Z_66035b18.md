# Two-circle region geometry (real-roots-tc4-region)

**Accomplished**

- New file `HexRealRootsMathlib/TwoCircleRegion.lean` (namespace
  `HexRealRootsMathlib.TwoCircle`), sorry-free, appended to the umbrella:
  - `InTwoCircle a b w`: the open two-circle region as the algebraic condition
    `0 < s.re ∨ 3·s.re² < s.im²` on the Möbius seed `s = (w−a)·conj(b−w)`.
  - `not_inTwoCircle_iff_mem_sector`: for `w ≠ b`, region complement ↔ Möbius
    image `(w−a)/(b−w)` in the closed sector `{z | z.re ≤ −‖z‖/2}` (consumer
    contract for the variation count).
  - `not_inTwoCircle_self`: `b` itself is outside (seed vanishes).
  - `dist_le`: ball bound, region ⊆ closed ball of radius `√3(b−a)/2` at the
    midpoint.
  - `inTwoCircle_conj`: conjugation symmetry.
  - `inTwoCircle_ofReal`: real points of `(a, b)` are in the region.
  - `inTwoCircle_iff_disc`: full disc characterisation (union of the two open
    discs, centres `(a+b)/2 ± i(b−a)/(2√3)`, radius `(b−a)/√3`) — closed, not
    downgraded.
  - Concrete sanity `example`s at `a = 0, b = 2`.
- `lake build HexRealRootsMathlib` green (only pre-existing intentional sorry in
  `TwoCircle.lean`); `scripts/check_dag.py` clean.
