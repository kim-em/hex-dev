## Proof approach

The proof goes through the `columnSumMatrix` expansion of
`det_columnSumMatrix_eq_sum_columnTuples`:

1. `M * auxAdjugateM M = columnSumMatrix M ((auxAdjugateM M).transpose)`
   entrywise (commutativity of `R`).
2. `auxAdjugateM M = columnSumMatrix 1 ((auxAdjugateM M).transpose)`
   follows from `one_mul` plus the same `columnSumMatrix` identity.
3. Apply `det_columnSumMatrix_eq_sum_columnTuples` to expand the LHS as
   an ordered-column-tuple sum with `source := M`.
4. The private helper
   `det_columnTupleMatrix_eq_det_mul_det_one : det (columnTupleMatrix M cols)
   = det M * det (columnTupleMatrix 1 cols)` covers both the injective
   case (lift `cols` to a vector in `permutationVectors n`, apply
   `det_colPermute_vector`) and the non-injective case
   (`det_columnTupleMatrix_eq_zero_of_not_injective` on both sides).
5. `foldl_det_sum_mul_left_zero` then pulls `det M` out of the sum.
6. Reverse-rewriting `det_columnSumMatrix_eq_sum_columnTuples` and the
   `eq_columnSumMatrix_one_transpose` bridge gives `det M *
   det (auxAdjugateM M)`.
