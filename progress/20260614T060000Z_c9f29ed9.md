## Key finding (premise correction)

The directive framed `residualInvariant` as a uniformly-true `forall fuel`
provider to be "proven". It is genuinely **false at small fuel** (at fuel 0 the
loop residual is `g_inner = monicGcd f f'`, not derivative-zero). The correct
discharge is the *size-bounded* `_of_size_lt` lemma, valid because every
consumer (weighted and pairwise) already carries `f.size < fuel`. `hrawState`,
by contrast, is unconditionally true.
