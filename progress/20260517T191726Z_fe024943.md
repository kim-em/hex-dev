## Audit decisions (why exactly three tags)

- **Tagged** `polyProduct_singleton`, `polyProduct_empty`,
  `polyProduct_append`: free RHSs, no side conditions, stable
  patterns. Together they form the "fold polyProduct over array
  structure" leaf set.
- **Skipped** `polyProduct_singleton_append`: subsumed by the
  tagged `polyProduct_append` + `polyProduct_singleton` chain on
  `Array.polyProduct (#[g] ++ rest)`. Existing explicit usages in
  `simpa [..., polyProduct_singleton_append]` calls still succeed
  (the lemma stays available; it just no longer needs to be passed
  explicitly).
- **Skipped** `polyProduct_cons_toArray`: subsumed by
  `List.toArray_cons` (already simp in Lean core) + the tagged
  `polyProduct_append` + `polyProduct_singleton` chain.
- **Skipped** `one_mul_zpoly`: downstream callers in
  `HexBerlekampZassenhaus/Basic.lean` use `rw [..., ZPoly.one_mul_zpoly]`
  in 14+ places, indicating the current normal form does NOT
  auto-collapse `1 * g` for `ZPoly`. The issue body explicitly
  warned to skip this if downstream relies on a different normal
  form.
