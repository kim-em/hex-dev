### Implementation note

The issue's implementation hint for prefix-none suggested driving the
contradiction through the unscaled-vs-scaled candidate gap. In
practice the chain factors cleanly through the existing monic
proof skeleton because the executable `recombinationCandidate d T` is
always monic (from `recombinationCandidate_monic` under monic lifted
factors), and the deep UFD argument only needs candidate-side
monicness, not core-side monicness. The only place core monicness
genuinely entered was the per-factor natDegree identity (now solved
by the primitive natDegree variant), which makes the rest of the
chain trivially mechanical.
