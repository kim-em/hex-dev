## Accomplished

In `HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/BadVectorAuxiliary.lean`:

- `coeff_auxiliaryPolynomialWithCorrections_high_decomp`: for an in-range
  coordinate `j`, scaling the corrected auxiliary-polynomial coefficient by the
  per-coordinate cut modulus `p ^ ℓ_j` (`ℓ_j = bhksCoeffCutThreshold p input j`)
  recovers the uncut CLD residue structure. Each lifted-factor term
  `vec_i * cldCoeffs_i` becomes `vec_i` times the high part
  `centeredResiduePow p k q_{i,j} − centeredResiduePow p ℓ_j (centeredResiduePow p k q_{i,j})`
  of the quotient coefficient, and the diagonal correction term stays explicit
  (now carrying the extra `p ^ ℓ_j` factor). The proof rewrites the coefficient
  via the existing `coeff_auxiliaryPolynomialWithCorrections`, distributes the
  scalar across the CLD fold, and substitutes the executable decomposition per
  factor.

- `foldl_add_mul_eq` (private): scalar distribution over a left fold whose step
  adds `g i` — `c * foldl (·+g i) a = foldl (·+h i) (c*a)` when `c*g i = h i`
  pointwise. Used to push the `p ^ ℓ_j` weight inside the CLD fold.

The `p ^ ℓ_j` weight is kept deliberately: per deliverable 3, `cldCoeffs` is the
high-bit quotient `psiCut`, not a residue representative of the quotient
coefficient modulo `p ^ k`. The lemma does not claim any such congruence.
