## Design decisions

- **`S * M` over iterated `updateCol`.** Packaging the reduction as a
  right-multiplication by one explicit upper-triangular matrix replaces
  the inductive "process pivots in decreasing order" bookkeeping with
  `det_mul` + `det_of_upperTriangular`. The natural-order Fin layout
  makes the transform genuinely upper triangular (left-block support of
  `-a X^t` sits below `m`; right-block support of `b X^t` is capped at
  `(n-d)+t`), so no reordering is needed.
- **General form, not monic-only.** The determinant carries the honest
  cofactor leading coefficient `(b.coeff (n-d)) ^ d`; under a monic
  cofactor this is `1` and the determinant is preserved. #6858 chooses.
- **Proof-free matrix definition.** `colReduceTransform`'s pivot entries
  are raw `coeff` formulas, decoupled from the `degreeLT` membership
  proofs; a separate `basisProd_repr_*` lemma reconnects to the syzygy
  lemma. Keeps the def clean and the triangular/diagonal proofs
  arithmetic-only.
- `set_option maxHeartbeats 400000` on the capstone: the goal carries
  the full Sylvester-of-products term, so tactic steps are heavy against
  the shared per-declaration budget. No `sorry`/`axiom`/`native_decide`.
