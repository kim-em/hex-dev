## Current frontier

- The main unresolved point is not whether fixed benchmarks reuse the
  warmup child across measured repeats; they do not.
- The real design question is which API `lean-bench` should offer for
  repeated evaluation of canonical workloads without accidentally
  benchmarking a cached closed value: stricter `IO`/thunk-only fixed
  registration, a separate thunk benchmark form, or stronger docs/warnings.
