## Accomplished

- Added the private array-level structural lemma
  `DensePoly.divModArrayAux_eq_of_polynomial_mul` in `HexPoly/Euclid.lean`.
  It states that under (i) array `q` whose top entry is the divisor's leading
  coefficient `lc ≠ 0`, (ii) a scaling function `scaleLead` that exactly
  recovers `a` from `a * lc`, and (iii) no zero divisors of `lc`, the
  `divModArrayAux` loop terminates with quotient `quot + m` and remainder `0`
  whenever the polynomial-multiple invariant `ofCoeffs rem = m * ofCoeffs q`
  holds at entry. Proof is by induction on `fuel`, peeling one leading
  monomial of `m` per step; the bound `B` parameterises the size of the
  remaining factor so the induction lets the IH instantiate at a strictly
  smaller value.
