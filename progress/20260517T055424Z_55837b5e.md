### Design notes

* The issue body anticipated this would require a Berlekamp-level
  `berlekampFactor_factors_pairwise_coprime` theorem and estimated
  ~200–300 lines.  Final size: ~83 lines in `HexBerlekamp/Factor.lean`
  + ~366 lines in `HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/Basic.lean` = ~449
  lines, within the upward-revised range the issue acknowledged.
* The `(berlekampFactor f).factors.Pairwise` strengthening was not
  inlined into the recursive predicate proof: instead the proof works
  directly from `factorProduct factors ∣ X squarefree`.  The Pairwise
  form remains as a separately useful umbrella for any caller that
  needs pairwise statements without the recursive split structure.
* The `factorsModP_coprime` proof does **not** require `hcore_monic`
  (unlike the `polyProduct_congr` sibling decomposed as #4568).  The
  coprime structure of the Berlekamp output is independent of the
  scaling distinction between `modP p core` and
  `monicModularImage (modP p core)` — both are nonzero and divide each
  other up to a unit — so the no-squared invariant transports through
  the monic modular image without needing the core to be monic.
