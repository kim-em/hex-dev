# Issue #4223 — direct one-way Rabin test soundness

## Accomplished

- Claimed feature issue #4223.
- Replaced the body of
  `HexBerlekampMathlib.rabinTest_true_irreducible` with a direct proof
  from `Hex.Berlekamp.rabinTest f hmonic = true` to
  `Irreducible (toMathlibPolynomial f)`. The proof no longer routes
  through the `rabin_irreducible` iff theorem.
- Added a fully proven Rabin substrate
  `HexBerlekampMathlib.Rabin.irreducible_dvd_frobeniusPolynomial_of_natDegree_dvd`
  to discharge the missing converse-direction divisibility step:
  for irreducible `g : Polynomial (ZMod p)` with
  `g.natDegree ∣ N`, `g ∣ X^(p^N) - X`. The proof uses
  `AdjoinRoot.powerBasis`, `FiniteField.pow_finrank_eq_natCard`, and
  `FiniteField.pow_card_pow` to land the divisibility in `AdjoinRoot g`,
  then `AdjoinRoot.mk_eq_zero` to transport back to the polynomial ring.
- Main proof flow: decompose `rabinTest` via
  `Rabin.rabinTest_true_to_mathlib_checks`, then for any factorisation
  `fM = a * b` with both factors nonunit, extract an irreducible
  `g ∣ a` via `WfDvdMonoid.exists_irreducible_factor`. The
  `natDegree g ∣ n`, `natDegree g < n` chain is closed by
  `Hex.Berlekamp.exists_maximalProperDivisor_dvd`, and the Rabin
  coprimality leg at the chosen maximal proper divisor combined with
  the new substrate forces `g` to be a unit, contradicting
  irreducibility.
- `checkIrreducibilityCertificate_irreducible` is unchanged and still
  routes through `rabinTest_true_irreducible`.
