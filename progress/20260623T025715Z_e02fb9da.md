## Why the premise is wrong

`coreLiftData core B pd` and `toMonicLiftData core B pd` do not share
`liftedFactors`. Both route through `henselLiftData`, which Hensel-lifts the
*same* `pd.factorsModP` against the **target** passed in
(`Basic.lean:2694-2700`): `coreLiftData` targets `monicTarget core p a`
(`≡ core·ℓf⁻¹`), `toMonicLiftData` targets `(toMonic core).monic`
(`c^(n-1)·core(X/c)`). Different non-congruent targets ⇒ different lifted
factors ⇒ different `bhksLatticeBasis` / `bhksProjectedRows` / RREF
`bhksEquivalenceClassIndicators`. The two recovery loops iterate over
different indicator arrays and different selected subsets, so the #8298
per-candidate bridge (`RecoveredAtLiftM1.candidate_eq`, fixed `d`/`S`) has no
per-selection correspondence to lift through the loop's `map` — the route the
issue proposes does not apply.
