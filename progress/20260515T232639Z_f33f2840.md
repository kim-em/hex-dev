# 2026-05-15 23:26 UTC — Berlekamp completeness composition (f33f2840)

## Accomplished

Added the algebraic completeness theorem `irreducible_of_no_kernelWitnessSplit_squareFree`
in `HexBerlekamp/RabinSoundness.lean`: for a monic square-free `f ∈ F_p[x]`,
if every fixed-space kernel witness yields `kernelWitnessSplit? f w = none`,
then `f` is irreducible.

The proof composes:
- A monic-coprime split via `exists_monic_irreducible_factor_of_factor` (`g`
  monic irreducible factor of `a₀`, with `f / g` monic of positive degree).
- A new Bezout-coefficient bridge
  `exists_bezout_eq_one_of_gcd_dvd_one`: from `gcd a b ∣ 1`, scale the xgcd
  Bezout identity by the cofactor of `1` to obtain explicit `s, t` with
  `s * a + t * b = 1`. This avoids needing `Monic (DensePoly.gcd a b)` as
  a hypothesis, which the existing
  `exists_reduced_crtZeroOne_kernelWitness_of_squareFree_split` requires.
- A new Bezout-version CRT witness theorem
  `exists_reduced_crtZeroOne_kernelWitness_of_bezout`, parallel to
  `exists_reduced_crtZeroOne_kernelWitness_of_coprime_split` but consuming
  arbitrary Bezout coefficients rather than `gcd a b = 1`.
- A combined wrapper
  `exists_reduced_crtZeroOne_kernelWitness_of_squareFree_monic_split` that
  feeds these together for a square-free monic split.
- The fixed-space iff
  `isFixedSpaceKernelPolynomial_iff_dvd_linearPow_sub_self` (#4314) to make
  `h` an algebraic kernel polynomial.
- The spanning lemma `fixedSpaceKernelPolynomial_coeffVector_complete`
  (#4034) to decompose `h`'s coefficient vector as a linear combination of
  basis polynomial coefficient vectors.
- A new local helper `nullspaceBasisMatrix_entry_eq_basis_coeff` relating
  `nullspaceBasisMatrix` entries to basis polynomial coefficients.
- A polynomial-level argument that if every basis polynomial has size
  `≤ 1`, then `h` is itself a constant (extracted via a fresh variable
  `d` to avoid recursion in `rw`).
- The matrix→algebraic direction of #4314 applied to a nonconstant basis
  polynomial.
- `dvd_primeFieldProduct_witness_of_dvd_linearPow_sub_self` (#4160) to
  lift `f ∣ linearPow w p - w` to the witness product.
- `exists_kernelWitnessSplit?_some_of_witnessProduct_dvd_of_pos_degree`
  (#4064) to derive `kernelWitnessSplit? f w = some _`, contradicting
  `hno_split`.

Also added the composition theorem `berlekampFactor_singleton_irreducible`
in the same module, combining
`kernelWitnessSplit?_none_of_berlekampFactor_factors_length_le_one`
(`HexBerlekamp/Factor.lean`) with the algebraic completeness theorem above.
