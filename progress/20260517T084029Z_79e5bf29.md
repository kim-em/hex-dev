## Accomplished

**#4602 (HO-1 substrate, Mathlib structural radical-relation between
`squareFreeCore` and `repeatedPart`)** — decomposed into two sub-issues.

Scoping showed the full deliverable requires ~300-500 lines spanning
several orthogonal proof techniques, far beyond a single-session budget.
The proof would need:

1. Bridge from executable `repeatedPart = ratPolyPrimitivePart (gcd
   ratPrimitive derivative)` to Mathlib `gcd` over `Polynomial ℚ`. Uses
   `HexPolyMathlib.toPolynomial_gcd_associated` and
   `ratPolyPrimitivePart_rational_associate`.

2. Char-0 polynomial fact `gcd(p, p') ~ divRadical p` over `Polynomial
   K` for `K` a field of characteristic zero. **Not in Mathlib** — needs
   a multiplicity argument: in char 0, `v_q(p') = v_q(p) - 1` for `v_q(p)
   ≥ 1`, so `v_q(gcd(p, p')) = v_q(p) - 1 = v_q(divRadical p)`.
   Mathlib has `divRadical_dvd_derivative` (the forward inclusion) at
   `Mathlib/RingTheory/Polynomial/Radical.lean:35` but not the converse
   `gcd ∣ divRadical`.

3. Gauss descent: every irreducible factor of `R` (in `Polynomial ℤ`,
   primitive) corresponds to an irreducible factor of `R.map ℚ` (Gauss
   for primitive polynomials).

4. From `S_Q ~ radical P_Q` over `ℚ` + squarefreeness of `S` over `ℤ`,
   conclude every irreducible factor of `R` divides `S` over `Polynomial
   ℤ`, hence `R ∣ S^N` for some `N`.

5. UFD peeling: extract exponents `e_q` such that `R = u * ∏ q^{e_q}`
   for some unit `u`.

6. Sign-normalisation: constrain `u = 1` via
   `primitiveSquareFreeDecomposition_leadingCoeff_repeatedPart_nonneg`
   and `squareFreeCore_leadingCoeff_pos_of_ne_zero`.

7. Bridge back to `Hex.ZPoly` via `HexPolyZMathlib.equiv.injective`.

The decomposition splits at a natural seam — divisibility (steps 1-4)
versus exponent extraction (steps 5-7):
