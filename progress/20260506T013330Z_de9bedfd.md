In `HexPolyFp/QuotientFrobenius.lean`:

Quotient-side homomorphism lemmas:

- `Quotient.add_eq_reduce_val`, `mul_eq_reduce_val` — quotient sum/product
  equals reduction of the underlying FpPoly sum/product.
- `Quotient.reduce_add`, `reduce_mul` — `reduce (f + h) = reduce f +
  reduce h` and the multiplicative analogue.
- `Quotient.pow_eq_reduce_linearPow` — quotient `n`-th power equals the
  reduction of `linearPow` applied to the canonical representative.
- `Quotient.mul_pow` — `(a * b)^n = a^n * b^n` on the quotient (general
  exponent, by induction on `n`).

Quotient Frobenius (deliverable 1):

- `Quotient.add_pow_prime` — `(a + b)^p = a^p + b^p` on the quotient,
  lifted from `FpPoly.linearPow_add_prime` via `reduce_add`.
- `Quotient.add_pow_pPow` — iterated Freshman's dream
  `(a + b)^(p^k) = a^(p^k) + b^(p^k)` for every `k`, by induction on `k`.

Constant Fermat (deliverable 2):

- `Quotient.linearPow_C_pow_prime` — `linearPow (C c) p = C c` over
  `F_p`, derived from a private induction `linearPow_C : linearPow (C c)
  n = C (c^n)` plus `ZMod64.pow_prime`.
- `Quotient.reduce_C_pow_prime_eq` — `(reduce (C c))^p = reduce (C c)`.
- `Quotient.reduce_C_pow_pPow_eq` — iterated `(reduce (C c))^(p^k) =
  reduce (C c)`, by induction on `k`.

X-generation capstone (deliverable 3):

- `Quotient.X_pow_pPowN` — under hypothesis `X^(p^n) = X`, every power
  `X^m` is fixed by the Frobenius iterate.
- `Quotient.reduce_monomial_eq` — `reduce (monomial m c) = reduce (C c)
  * X^m` in the quotient, via the FpPoly identity `monomial m c = C c *
  X^m`.
- Private polynomial-decomposition helpers `fpPolyMonoSum`,
  `quotMonoSum`, `coeff_fpPolyMonoSum`, `fpPolyMonoSum_eq_self`,
  `reduce_fpPolyMonoSum`, `reduce_eq_quotMonoSum` — give the

- Private `quotMonoSum_pow_pPow_eq_self` — under `X^(p^n) = X`, every
