## Accomplished

Claimed #6815 and added `exists_factor_of_modPIndex` in
`HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/Basic.lean`.

The theorem works in the successful `choosePrimeData? core = some primeData`
branch: for any `ModPFactorIndex primeData`, it extracts a normalized integer
factor `g` of `core`, proves `Irreducible (toPolynomial g)` and `g ∣ core`,
and transports the selected modular factor divisibility into
`toMathlibPolynomial (monicModPImage (ZPoly.modP primeData.p g))`.

Key proof route:

- the indexed modular factor divides the full modular product;
- that product is the monic modular image of `core`, which divides the raw
  mod-`p` image of `core`;
- divisibility is moved to `(toPolynomial core).map (Int.castRingHom (ZMod p))`;
- `UniqueFactorizationMonoid.normalizedFactors` of `toPolynomial core` supplies
  the integer factor after a prime-divides-list-product step;
- the final monic-image target is handled by a zero/nonzero split on
  `ZPoly.modP p g`.
