The diagnostic chain:

* The directive's strategy via `schurSigma_foldl_eq_noPivotCorrection_zero`
  + `schurSigma_noPivotCorrection_succ` (lines 7067, 7167) does yield a
  Sylvester / fraction-free Gauss chain identity for the non-singular
  case: `schurSigma rows_Schur a (p+1) = noPivotLoop p.diag *
  gramMatrix[a][p+1] - noPivotLoop (p+1).matrix[a][p+1]`. Diagonal `i = j`
  and strict-lower `j < i` non-singular both fall out cleanly from this.
* The singular case for `j > s + 1` cascades through the IH at columns
  `(s, j-1]`, where `rows[p][p] = 0` and `rows[i][p] = 0` (Bareiss
  column-major loop early-terminates), collapsing the σ-chain to zero
  step by step.
* The **boundary case `j = s + 1`** fails. The chain ends at
  `p_iter = s` with `denom = rows[s-1][s-1] = noPivotLoop (s-1).matrix
  [s-1][s-1]` non-zero (last non-singular pivot), and the numerator is
  `rows[i][s] * rows[s+1][s] = noPivotLoop s.matrix[i][s] *
  noPivotLoop s.matrix[s+1][s]`. The Bareiss-side characterisation
  `scaledCoeffRows_eq_zero_of_singularStep_lt` gives `scaledCoeffRows
  [i][s+1] = 0`, so closing the sorry requires the product to be zero.
* That product **is** zero by `leadingPrefix_gram_zero_pivot_column_zero_
  of_singular_step` (line 4273), but that lemma takes `hquot :
  BareissGramInitialRegularStepQuotientProvider b`. The
  `HexGramSchmidt/Int.lean` docstring on the abstraction
  (line 3832-3838) explicitly states concrete providers are constructed
  only in `HexGramSchmidtMathlib`, where Mathlib's Bareiss-Desnanot
  proof infrastructure is available; the Mathlib-free layer can only
  consume the abstraction.
* So closing the residual sorry as a Mathlib-free synthesis from the
  existing σ-correction helpers alone is structurally blocked. Two
  exit paths are open: a Mathlib-free PSD column-zero lemma (would
  itself require new infrastructure), or migration of `getArrayEntry_
  scaledCoeffRowsSchur_eq` and its consumers to the bridge layer with
  the redesigned witness from #6503 deliverable 1.

This is the same shape of gap that the existing #5655 sorry at line
5725 (`gramDetVecEntry_eq_leadingPrefix_bareiss`) tracks: the docstring
there says discharging requires "propagating quotient provenance
through this private theorem and its public consumers".
