# BHKS Lemma 5.1: unconditional CLD Phi coefficient bound (issue #6185)

## Accomplished

- Discharged the analytic hypotheses of `BHKS.abs_phi_coeff_le` and landed
  the unconditional `BHKS.abs_phi_coeff_le_of_monic_factor` in
  `HexBerlekampZassenhausMathlib/CLDColumnBound.lean`. The new theorem
  takes only `g.Monic`, the factorisation `f = g * h`, and a coefficient
  index `j`, and concludes
  `(Int.natAbs ((phi f g).coeff j) : ℝ) ≤
    (Nat.choose (f.natDegree - 1) j : ℝ) * (f.natDegree : ℝ) * l2norm f`.
- Used `Polynomial.derivative_eq_sum_rootDeletionDerivativeSummand` from
  `HexPolyZMathlib/RobinsonForm.lean` to decompose `(phi f g).map ℂ` over
  the complex roots of `g.map ℂ` as a multiset sum of
  `h.map ℂ * rootDeletionDerivativeSummand (g.map ℂ) α` summands.
- Per-summand Mahler bound via multiplicativity: each summand satisfies
  `M(h.map ℂ * rootDelDeriv ...) ≤ M(f.map ℂ)`, using
  `Polynomial.mahlerMeasure_rootDeletionDerivativeSummand_le` plus
  `M(g) ≥ 1` from `g` being monic over `ℤ`.
- Combined per-summand coefficient bound
  (`Polynomial.norm_coeff_le_choose_mul_mahlerMeasure`) with
  `norm_multiset_sum_le` and `Multiset.sum_le_card_nsmul` to bound the
  sum of `g.natDegree ≤ f.natDegree` summands.
- Added private helpers in the same file:
  `phi_map_eq_sum_h_mul_rootDeletionDerivativeSummand`,
  `mahlerMeasure_h_mul_rootDeletionDerivativeSummand_le`,
  `natDegree_h_mul_rootDeletionDerivativeSummand_le` (per-summand degree
  bound, restricted to `α ∈ roots`), and `phi_natDegree_le_of_monic_factor`
  (the global degree subtask).
- No new top-level imports beyond `HexPolyZMathlib.RobinsonForm` and
  `Mathlib.Algebra.BigOperators.Ring.Multiset` (for
  `Multiset.sum_map_mul_left`).
