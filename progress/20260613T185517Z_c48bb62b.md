## Key finding (premise gap for #6858)

The determinant factor is `(b.coeff (n-d))^d` where `b` is the **second
cofactor** (`g = q·b + C m·s`), i.e. `(leadingCoeff g)^d` under `q.Monic`.
`q.Monic` alone does **not** make this `1`. So #6858's literal deliverable
`(m^d) ∣ resultant` from only the four witnesses + degree conditions does not
follow — it needs `m` coprime to `leadingCoeff g` (true for `m = pᵏ`, `p ∤ lc`
in BZ) or a monic-`g` hypothesis. Counterexample: `m = 2`, `leadingCoeff g =
2`, `d = 1` yields only `2 ∣ 2·resultant`. Posted a heads-up on #6858 (which is
claimed by another agent) recommending the added side condition.
