- For non-monic `core` these are different, non-associate polynomials (coeff i is `lc^i · centered(product_i)` vs `centered(lc · product_i)`), so the seam cannot be patched by an equality rewrite — the model candidate must be redefined to the dilation shape.
- The model otherwise compiles self-consistently with the old scale shape (hence only one error), so redefining the candidate cascades through every lemma that unfolds/rewrites it (~30 recovery/coverage lemmas) and forces re-deriving the ~8 `RepresentsIntegerFactorAtLift` construction sites in the dilation model.
- `representsIntegerFactorAtLift_mul_of_monic_core` (`Basic.lean:8400`) and the existence machinery are monic-core only (`scale 1 = id`); the non-monic recovery needs a `ZPoly.dilate`↔`Polynomial.comp` correspondence that does not yet exist (confirmed: nothing in `HexPolyZMathlib/` maps `dilate`, and Mathlib has no ready `coeff_comp_C_mul_X`).
