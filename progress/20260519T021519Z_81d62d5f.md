## Accomplished

Replaced all 34 explicit `DensePoly.coeff_add _ _ _ <hyp>` /
`DensePoly.coeff_sub _ _ _ <hyp>` call sites in
`HexBerlekamp/RabinSoundness.lean` with the typeclass-specialized
`DensePoly.coeff_add_semiring` / `DensePoly.coeff_sub_ring` `@[simp]`
wrappers introduced in PR #5169 (fixed in #5188).

  hypotheses included inline `have hzero` / `have hzero_sub`
  declarations and the namespace-private `zmod64_zero_sub_zero`
  helper.

  at former lines 440, 743, 807, 1188, 1189, 1226, 1385, 1408. The
  long manual `hzero` proof attached to `primeFieldLinearFactor_coeff_one`

  definition (former lines 1565–1577) since no caller remained

its `grind` context fact when `hzero` was deleted; added a
`show (1 : ZMod64 p) - 0 = 1` step to give `grind` a concrete
target. Matches the `show ...; grind` adjustment pattern from
the merged sibling PR #5203.
