## Accomplished

Closed issue #1998 (`HexMatrix: python-flint oracle for det/RREF/nullspace/rank`):

- Added `HexMatrix/EmitFixtures.lean` — JSONL emit driver with nine
  square integer fixtures spanning `random/`, `singular/`, and
  `triangular/` shapes at 4×4, 6×6, and 8×8.  For each fixture the
  driver emits five `result` records: `det` (combinatorial),
  `bareiss` (fraction-free), `rank` (rational), `rref` (rational
  echelon as `[num, den]` pairs plus pivot columns), and `nullspace`
  (rational basis vectors).
- Added `scripts/oracle/matrix_flint.py` — python-flint driver
  cross-checking each op via `fmpz_mat.det/rank` and `fmpq_mat.rref`.
  Nullspace is verified against basis-independent invariants
  (cardinality matches `m - rank`, every Lean basis vector is
  annihilated over `Q`, basis vectors are linearly independent), so
  Lean's basis is free to differ from FLINT's column basis up to an
  invertible transform.
- Added committed fixture
  `conformance-fixtures/HexMatrix/matrix.jsonl` (54 records, 9 cases ×
  6 records each).
- Wired `hexmatrix_emit_fixtures` into `lakefile.lean` and into the
  `oracle-pyflint` job in `.github/workflows/conformance.yml` (build
  exe, diff against committed fixture, pipe into oracle).
