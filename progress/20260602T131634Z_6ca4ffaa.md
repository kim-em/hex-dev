## Accomplished

- Claimed #6335 (directive: rewrite `Hex.LLLState.setEntry` to avoid forced
  row-COW).
- Added top-level `Vector.modify` in `HexMatrix/Basic.lean`, a thin wrapper
  over `Array.modify` so the swap-with-placeholder ownership-transfer
  pattern survives codegen on `Vector α n`.
- Rewrote `Hex.LLLState.setEntry` (`HexLLL/Basic.lean:79`) from
  `M.set i ((M.get i).set j x)` to `M.modify i (·.set j x)`. The previous
  form forced a `lean_inc` on the borrowed inner row, so every entry
  update hit the COW path in `lean_array_fset` and copied the row.
- Verified the new IR. `lp_Hex___private_HexLLL_Basic_0__Hex_LLLState_setEntry___redArg`
  in `.lake/build/ir/HexLLL/Basic.c` now does the
  `lean_array_fget` → `lean_box(0)` placeholder → in-place `fset` →
  put-back sequence with no `lean_inc` on the inner row.
- Re-benched on `carica` (M2 Ultra), HEAD vs HEAD+patch (3 repeats each,
  `hexlll_bench run` medians):

  | target                 | baseline | patched | Δ      |
  |------------------------|---------:|--------:|-------:|
  | randomBoundedNormSq120 |  1.749 s | 1.609 s | −8.0 % |
  | randomBoundedNormSq150 |  3.500 s | 3.281 s | −6.3 % |
  | randomBoundedNormSq180 |  6.478 s | 6.051 s | −6.6 % |

  The n=120 row sits in the directive's expected −6 to −10 % range; the
  improvement holds at the larger ladder rungs too.
- Regenerated `reports/figures/hex-lll-comparator-{random-bounded,harsh-cubic}.svg`
  with the new bench data; the Lean curve in the random-bounded plot
  shifts downward as predicted.
- Updated `scripts/plots/hex-lll-comparator.py` to point at the new
  bench exports (`hex-lll-setentry-modify-{densified,harsh-cubic}.json`),
  preserving the existing Isabelle entries by merging with the prior
