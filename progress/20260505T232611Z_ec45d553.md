## Accomplished

Implemented #2413 in `HexGF2/Field.lean`:

- `GF2Poly.ofBoolListFrom`: recursive coefficient-list-to-packed-polynomial
  builder, parameterized by a starting degree, summing `monomial start` for
  each `true` entry.
- `GF2Poly.ofBoolList`: top-level wrapper at `start = 0`.
- `coeff_ofBoolListFrom_lt`: coefficients below `start` are `false`.
- `coeff_ofBoolListFrom_ge`: coefficients at indices `≥ start` read the
  matching list entry (defaulting to `false` past the end).
- `coeff_ofBoolList`: specializes to `(ofBoolList bs).coeff n = (bs[n]?).getD false`.
- `coeff_ofBoolList_length_le`: convenience corollary for indices at or above
  the length.
- `ofBoolList_isZero_or_degree_lt`: the constructed polynomial is zero or has
  degree strictly below the input list length, matching the
  `val_reduced` predicate of `GF2nPoly`.

Verified `lake build HexGF2.Field`, `lake build HexGF2`,
`python3 scripts/check_dag.py`, `git diff --check`, and a forbidden-token scan
