## Accomplished

- Added `HexHensel/CrossCheck.lean` covering 60 linear-vs-quadratic
  multifactor Hensel lift cross-checks: 5 fixtures × 4 precisions ×
  3 primes.
  - Fixtures: `factorsFromConstants` over distinct residues, with
    factor counts 3, 3, 4, 4, 5 per prime.
  - `f := ∏ factors + p` so the lift performs genuine work
    (`∏ factors ≠ f` over `Z`, `∏ factors ≡ f (mod p)`).
  - Precisions: `k ∈ {2, 4, 8, 16}` for `p ∈ {5, 7, 11}`.
  - Each `#guard` asserts
    `reduceArrModPow (multifactorLift p k f factors) p k`
    `= reduceArrModPow (multifactorLiftQuadratic p k f factors) p k`.
- Added a deliberate-corruption sanity check (`perturbedFirstFactor`)
  that confirms a `+1` shift on a single lifted factor diverges the
  two paths; verified manually that disabling the perturbation makes
  the negative `≠` guard fail at elaboration.
- Wired `import HexHensel.CrossCheck` into `HexHensel.lean` so
  `lake build HexHensel` elaborates the new fixtures.
