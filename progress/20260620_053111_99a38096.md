# HexPolyFp public-theorem docstrings (#8200)

## Accomplished

Closed the theorem-docstring gap, one of the two residual Phase 6
exit criteria for `HexPolyFp` (the other being SquareFree dead code,
a separate issue). Added `/--` docstrings to all 27 undocumented
public theorems across the five files called out by the #8178 audit:

- **Basic.lean** (14): the ring-axiom restatements `add_zero`,
  `zero_add`, `neg_zero`, `add_left_neg`, `add_right_neg`, `sub_zero`,
  `zero_sub`, `sub_self`, `zero_mul`, `mul_zero`, `one_mul`, `mul_one`,
  plus `eval_one` and `linearPow_zero`. Kept these terse, matching the
  existing `add_comm`/`add_assoc` style ("Part of the commutative-ring
  structure on `FpPoly p`").
- **Quotient.lean** (4): `ext`, `elements_length` (notes it gives the
  order `p ^ deg g` of the quotient ring), `add_zero`, `zero_add`.
- **Compose.lean** (5): `compose_zero`, `compose_C`, `compose_X`,
  `C_add_eq`, `compose_foldl_X_sub_C` (the linear-product transport law).
- **ModCompose.lean** (2): `composeModMonic_zero`, `composeModMonic_C`.
- **Enumeration.lean** (2): `coeffLists_zero`, `coeffLists_succ`.

## Decisions

- Documentation-only: diff is 38 pure insertions, zero deletions —
