- Claimed and executed feature issue #6074 (ordered four-row `B[p1]` row
  replacement transports in the HexMatrix Mathlib bridge layer).
- Added `OrderedFourShift.cycleAhead` private permutation helper in
  `HexMatrixMathlib/Determinant/Core.lean`: a cycle on positions
  `[a, a+m]` of `Fin (n+1)` defined recursively as a product of
  adjacent transpositions. Sign is `(-1)^m`.

  `matrixEquiv_setRow_p1_eq_submatrix_nMatrix`: under `matrixEquiv`,
  `setRow (nMatrix B p1 q) ⟨p_t.val - 1⟩ B[p1]` equals
  `(matrixEquiv (nMatrix B p_t q)).submatrix σ id` for the cycle
  `σ = cycleAhead p1.val (p_t.val - p1.val - 1)`.
- Proved the determinant-level helper
  `det_setRow_p1_eq_pow_mul_nDet`, combining
  `matrixEquiv_setRow_p1_eq_submatrix_nMatrix`,
  `Matrix.det_permute`, and `sign_cycleAhead` to identify the LHS
  with `(-1)^(p_t.val - p1.val - 1) * nDet B p_t q`.

  - `ordered_four_cofactorRowPairing_p2_p1_eq_pow_mul_nDet`
  - `ordered_four_cofactorRowPairing_p3_p1_eq_pow_mul_nDet`

  `Hex.Matrix.det_setRow_eq_cofactorRowPairing` and then applies the
  determinant-level helper.
