## Accomplished

Proved `IsRREF.nullspace_complete` in `HexMatrix/RREF.lean` (issue #3048),
removing the `sorry` that backed completeness of the executable nullspace
basis.

The proof follows the SPEC strategy:
1. Push `M * v = 0` through `transform_mul` and `mulVec_zero` to get
   `D.echelon * v = 0`.
2. Pick the witness `c k := v[freeCols.get k]`.
3. Verify entry-wise via `colPartition`. The free case uses
   `nullspaceMatrix_free`/`_free_ne` and the indicator-fold lemma. The pivot
   case uses a freeSum/pivot identity derived from
   `(D.echelon * v)[pivotRow i] = 0`, decomposed by the perm
   `finRange m ~ pivotCols.toList ++ freeColsList`.

To avoid dependent-type rewriting issues with `Vector.ext`'s Nat/bound form,
the entry-wise lemma is proved for an arbitrary `Fin m` first, then
specialized — this lets us use `subst` on the `colPartition` hypothesis.
