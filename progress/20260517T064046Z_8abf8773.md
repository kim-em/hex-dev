### The blocker

The recently-landed `factorsModP_polyProduct_congr_*` (#4573) and
`factorsModP_coprime_*` (#4574) both internally invoke
`prod_berlekampFactor` and pin
`primeData.factorsModP = (berlekampFactor ...).factors.toArray` to derive
`factorProduct factors = modP_core`.  Under Option 3 the right-hand-side
of that equation becomes `(factors.map monicModularImage).toArray`,
which breaks both proofs at the `rw [heq]` step.  Closing this gap
requires:

1. Promoting `Hex.Berlekamp.leadingCoeff_mul_fpoly` from `private` to
   public (one keyword change in `HexBerlekamp/RabinSoundness.lean`).
2. A new `monicModularImage_mul` lemma in `HexBerlekampZassenhaus/Basic.lean`
   stating `monicModularImage (a * b) = monicModularImage a * monicModularImage b`
   for nonzero inputs.  The body is `~30 lines` of `scale` /
   `C_mul_eq_scale` manipulation plus an inline `(xy)⁻¹ = x⁻¹y⁻¹` in
   `ZMod64` (the latter is not currently in the codebase as a named
   lemma).
3. A `factorProduct_map_monicModularImage_eq_monicModularImage_factorProduct`
   inductive lemma chaining (2) through `factorProduct_cons`.
4. Applying (3) + `monicModularImage_modP_eq_of_monic` to conclude
   `factorProduct (raw.map monicModularImage) = modP_core` in the
   `polyProduct_congr` and `coprime` updates.
