- `{docstring}` on a `structure` requires docstrings on *all* its fields,
  not just the structure — a Verso constraint worth remembering for future
  chapters documenting record types.

  code span or the `[` escaped as `\[` — Verso parses bare brackets as a
  markdown link target. Wrapped the formula in backticks, matching the
  GFqRing chapter.
