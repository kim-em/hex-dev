# 2026-05-04 05:46Z — feature session, issue #2015

## Accomplished

- Closed #2015 by bumping `HexPoly/Conformance.lean` fixtures from
  degrees 2-3 toward the SPEC `core` ceiling (degrees 8-12).
  Highlights:
  - `polyTypical = 3 - 2x^2 + x^12` (degree 12)
  - `polyAdversarial = 4x - 5x^3 + 6x^12` with three trailing-zero
    raw coefficients to exercise normalization
  - `monomialAdversarial = -2x^12` overlaps with the degree-12 term
    of `polyAdversarial`, preserving the additive-interaction tests
  - Division typical: dividend `x^12 - 1` divided by `x - 1` gives
    the telescoping quotient `1 + x + ... + x^11`
  - Division adversarial: non-monic divisor `2x + 1` produces a
    fractional remainder `7/2` from a hand-built dividend
  - GCD typical: `(x-1)(x^11+1)` and `(x-1)(x^11-1)` — both monic
    degree 12, EEA terminates in two steps with `gcd = 2x - 2`
  - GCD adversarial: `(x^4+1)(x^8+2x^3+5)` and `x^4+1` — `R | L`
    pattern, gcd = R after one divMod step
  - Content fixtures: degree-12 primitive `1 + 5x^3 - 2x^7 + 3x^12`
    and `6 ×` it for nontrivial-content
  - CRT moduli bumped to coprime `1 + x^12` / `x^12 - 1` with
    Bezout `s = 1/2`, `t = -1/2` and witness `6 + x^12`
- Every `#guard` literal derived by hand from the polynomial
  algebra (telescoping factorization, EEA trace, `polyCRT`
  formula `u*t*b + v*s*a`), satisfying SPEC's ban on
  run-and-paste literals (commit 0355ce1).
- Verified deliberate corruption of `eval polyTypical 2 = 4091`
  to `4092` trips the build, then reverted.
