recovered-candidate pipeline that mirrors the Mathlib-side
`liftedRecoveryCandidate` / `RecoveredAtLift`:

- `toMonic (2X²+3X+1) = X²+3X+2 = (X+1)(X+2)`. The guard lifts the centred
  monic-transform factor `X+1`, dilates by `coreLc = 2`, primitivises and

  `exactQuotient? core` rejects (`none`).
- It runs `scaledRecombinationSearchMod 2 core 9 [X+1, X+2]` end-to-end and
  checks the recovered factors multiply back to `core`.
