* **Two helpers are NOT hoistable** —
  `zpoly_primitive_of_monic` and
  `zpoly_normalize_factor_sign_of_monic` are defined as
  `(monic_primitive_sign_normalized_of_monic h).2.1` and `.2.2`
  respectively, so they are destructure-projection corollaries
  of the very theorem whose body is the rewire target. Hoisting
  them above line 4473 is structurally impossible.
* **Practical rewire scope is even narrower than the partial
  hoist.** Of the body's four addressable inline blocks, only the
  13-line `hcs_pos` block at lines 4479-4491 has a rewire site
  (onto `zpoly_size_pos_of_monic`). The content-1 and
  normalizeFactorSign cases (lines 4497-4509 and 4510-4515) would
  be circular if rewired onto the cluster helpers.
  `zpoly_lc_pos_of_monic` is hoistable but has no rewire site:
  the body uses `leadingCoeff = 1`, not `0 < leadingCoeff`.
* **Two-thirds of the self-derivation gap cannot be closed by
  hoist alone.** The remaining content-1 and normalizeFactorSign
  inline blocks need an inversion of the cluster's proof direction
  (move their derivations into the helper bodies, make
  `monic_primitive_sign_normalized_of_monic` *call* them) — out of
  scope for a simple hoist + rewire cycle and explicitly deferred
  in the audit.

  4473. The drafted follow-up therefore declares
