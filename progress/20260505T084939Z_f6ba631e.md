## Accomplished

Claimed #2295 and discharged the `exists_irreducible_factor_of_factor`
proof leaf in `HexGF2/RabinSoundness.lean`. The lemma states that any
factor `a` of positive degree contains an irreducible divisor `g` with
`0 < deg g ≤ deg a`.

Strong-induction descent on `a.degree`:

- **Base / irreducible case**: if `Irreducible a` already holds, take
  `g = a` and use `dvd_refl'` for divisibility. The `0 < deg a` and
  `deg a ≤ deg a` legs are immediate.
- **Reducible case**: extract a nontrivial factorization `x * y = a`
  with `x.degree ≠ 0` and `y.degree ≠ 0`. Since `Irreducible a` is a
  `∀`-quantified Prop and the codebase avoids Mathlib, the existential
  witness is pulled out classically via `Classical.byContradiction` and
  two `by_cases` on whether each factor's degree is zero. Apply the IH
  to `x` (whose degree is strictly less than `a.degree` by the existing
  `factor_degree_lt`) to obtain an irreducible `g ∣ x`, hence `g ∣ a`
  by `dvd_trans`. Combine `deg g ≤ deg x ≤ deg a` for the bound.
