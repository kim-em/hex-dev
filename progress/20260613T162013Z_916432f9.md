**#6839 (diagnosed, skipped → replan)** — `RepresentsIntegerFactorAtLift`
dilation migration. Determined the directive's predicate shape (dilated-product
modular congruence `reduceModPow (dilate lc product) = reduceModPow factor`)
cannot route through #6838's recovery theorem
`liftedRecoveryCandidate_eq_factor_of_congruence_of_bound`, which consumes the
*unscaled* congruence to a `monicFactor` plus the dilate keystone
`primitivePart (dilate lc monicFactor) = factor`. The dilated-product congruence
carries neither the monic-factor congruence nor its Mignotte bound, and the
content-power gap (`dilate lc (centeredLift product)` carries `lc^i` content)
blocks the alternate route. Posted concrete type-level evidence + a corrected
existential predicate shape; left claimable for an owner decision on the public
`HenselSubsetCorrespondenceHypotheses` contract.

**#6857 (investigated, skipped → replan)** — Sylvester monic common-factor
column transform. Worked out the column semantics, the m-divisible-combination
identity `f·(bX^t) + g·(-a X^t) = m·(rb - sa)·X^t` (from the integer witnesses),
and the core difficulty: the determinant-preserving transform needs unit pivots,
which the naive syzygy (`lc(a)=lc(f)`, `lc(b)=lc(g)`) does not supply — the unit
pivots must come from monic `q`'s unitriangular change of basis (subresultant
PRS). Posted full notes + a suggested 2-way split. Sound issue; released for a
dedicated session.
