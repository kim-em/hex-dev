## Current frontier

The HO-22 deliverables are complete. The informational ratios
sit roughly flat around 5-6x for `add`/`sub`/`derivative` (Hex
faster on the algorithm; FLINT carries higher constant factors
plus driver startup), about 2.3x for `primitive_part`, and
about 1.4x adjusted for `content`. The `compose` surface is the
outlier — FLINT pulls steadily ahead from 0.505x at `n = 40` to
0.210x at `n = 64`, recorded as the first Concern. The `mul`
ladder has no eligible rung because FLINT's wall time at
`n = 128..512` sits near the driver-startup floor; the SPEC's
adjusted-ratio mechanism collapses to zero rather than
publishing a misleading verdict.
