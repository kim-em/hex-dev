`_of_choosePrimeData` on the constant-arm umbrella is documentary (the
constant branch fires before `choosePrimeData` is invoked, and the
signature has no `hchoose` premise). Three considerations:

1. The PR's docstring is explicit about this ("it is documentary here —
   the constant branch does not invoke `choosePrimeData`"), so a future
   reader is not surprised.
2. All sibling arms (small-mod singleton landed, quadratic / exhaustive
   in flight) use the `_of_choosePrimeData` suffix and consume a real
   `hchoose` premise. Uniformity of the family makes it easier for the
   eventual capstone assembly to dispatch over all arms by a single
   naming pattern.
3. Alternative names (`factor_constant_branch_entry_irreducible`,
   `factor_constant_branch_entry_irreducible_of_ne_zero`) would break
   the uniform suffix.

Defensible either way; leave as-is. Documented in review comment.
