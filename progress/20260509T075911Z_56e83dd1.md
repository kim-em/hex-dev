  `Rat`-specific cast arithmetic with the generic
  `Lean.Grind.Semiring.natCast_*` lemmas. Added at the bottom of
  `HexPoly/Euclid.lean`.
- In `HexPolyFp/SquareFree.lean`, added two new public theorems:
  - `strong_squareFree_characterization`: if `r` is square-free
    (`gcd r r' = 1`) and `g · g ∣ r`, then `g ∣ 1`. Proved via
    Leibniz expansion of `r' = (g · g · h)'` showing each summand is
    divisible by `g`, hence `g ∣ gcd r r' = 1`.
  - `gcd_quotient_common_divisor_dvd_one`: the saturation fact —
    if `r` is square-free and `g` divides both `r / gcd r d` and
    `d`, then `g ∣ 1`. Reduces to the strong characterization via
    `g ∣ gcd r d` and `g · g ∣ (r / gcd r d) · gcd r d = r`.
- Stated the saturation lemma as `g ∣ 1` (rather than the issue's
  candidate signature using `isUnitPolynomial`) so the new content
  lives at the `HexPolyFp` level — `isUnitPolynomial` is defined in
  `HexBerlekamp.Irreducibility`, which is downstream. The
  HexBerlekamp consumer can wrap with
  `isUnitPolynomial_of_dvd_isUnitPolynomial` and the existing
  `isUnitPolynomial_one`.
