### Choice of compositional route

The issue's "Sub-issue" discussion presented two routes: (a) strengthen
the umbrella signature to filter unit cores, or (b) use the existing
`reassemblePolynomialFactors_constant_irreducible_or_repeated_or_one`
classifier and rule out the `= 1` case via shouldRecord.  This PR uses
a third related route: the
`_mem_xPower_or_core_of_expansionComplete` classifier (used by the
singleton arm too) directly leverages `hcomplete` to rule out the
non-decomposed repeated-part fallback, leaving only the xPower /
singleton-core split.  The singleton-core case then becomes
`raw = squareFreeCore = 1`, contradicting `factorWithBound_entry_shouldRecord`.
This route mirrors the singleton arm's compositional structure most
closely and avoids the three-way case split the constant classifier
would force.
