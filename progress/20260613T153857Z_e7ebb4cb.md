# Progress: #6871 — normalized SquareFree tail support

## Accomplished

Claimed #6871 and isolated the first false-provider branch in
`HexPolyFp/SquareFree.lean`: raw derivative-active tails can be scalar
constants even when their normalized tail is `1`, so the executable residual
recursion must terminate without asking for raw
`squareFreeContributionReachable`.

Added closed support lemmas:

- `derivative_isZero_true_of_size_one`
- `pthRoot_eq_self_of_size_one`
- `squareFreeAuxRevContribution_size_one`

These show that any raw size-one residual has derivative zero, its `pthRoot`
is itself, and `squareFreeAuxRevContribution` contributes `1` after the fuel
countdown. This is the normalized-terminal case needed by the #6871 route.

Created follow-up #6872 for the remaining scalar-aware residual bridge.
