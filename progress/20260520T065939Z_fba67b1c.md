## Accomplished

Claimed #5627 and added the executable monicised-core surface in
`HexBerlekampZassenhaus/Basic.lean`.

The new `MonicisedCoreData` records the original core, leading coefficient,
degree, and monicised core. `MonicisedCoreData.ofCore` computes the standard
integer coefficient transform for non-monic inputs and is definitionally the
identity when the source leading coefficient is already `1`.

Added local sanity lemmas proving the transformed core is monic, preserves the
recorded degree in positive-degree cases, and is the identity for already-monic
input. Added `#guard` examples for a monic quadratic, the non-monic primitive
quadratic `2*x^2 + 3*x + 1`, and the zero edge case exposed by the API.
