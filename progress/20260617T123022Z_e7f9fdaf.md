### Recursive agreement helper (deliverable #2, with a corrected premise)

Added a private `multifactorLiftList_map_eq_quadratic` that inducts over the
factor list. The issue proposed a helper over targets congruent **modulo `p`**.
That premise is too weak: `hensel_unique` needs both lifted products to equal
the *same* target modulo `p ^ k`, and Hensel lifting is target-dependent above
the base prime, so mod-`p`-congruent targets give factors that differ mod
`p ^ k`. Mod-`p` congruence only suffices to transport the *invariant* (the
existing `quadraticMultifactorLiftInvariant_of_multifactorLiftInvariant_congr`),
not to pin the lifted factors.

The corrected helper carries **mod-`p ^ k`** target congruence. `hensel_unique`
then equates both the heads and the complements mod `p ^ k` at each split, and
the complement equality is exactly the recursive target congruence the
inductive call consumes. The public theorem instantiates the helper at
`f' = f` via `Hex.ZPoly.congr_refl`, so its signature is unchanged from the
issue (plus `hmonic`). The headline theorem is sound and fully proven; only the
internal decomposition's congruence level was corrected, per the project
directive guidance ("fix the decomposition, don't weaken the theorem").
