### Design notes

* Took option (b) from the issue body: extracted the analyzable
  `_of_some` branch rather than handling the `choosePrimeData? = none`
  fallback (`p = 3`).  The fallback case is left for the #4688 constructor
  wrapper.

* Proof core: each entry of `primeData.factorsModP` is
  `monicModularImage g'` for some raw Berlekamp factor `g'`.
  `irreducible_of_mem_berlekampFactor` gives
  `Irreducible (toMathlibPolynomial g')`; the transfer to
  `monicModularImage g'` routes through `toMathlibPolynomial_scale` —
  since `monicModularImage g' = scale (lc g')⁻¹ g'`, the Mathlib image
  equals `C ((lc g')⁻¹.toZMod) * toMathlibPolynomial g'`, a unit multiple
  of the original.  `Associated.irreducible` transfers the irreducibility
  through that unit factor.

* The square-freeness premise of `irreducible_of_mem_berlekampFactor`
  (on `monicModularImage (modP p core)`) is discharged via
  `gcd_monicModularImage_derivative_eq_one_local` applied to the modular
  square-freeness from `Hex.isGoodPrime_squareFreeModP`.
