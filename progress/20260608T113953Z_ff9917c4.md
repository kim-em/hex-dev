# Progress: integer Lovász ↔ rational `isLLLReduced` correspondence (#6589)

## Accomplished

- Added `Hex.HexLLLMathlib.lovasz_check_iff_isLLLReduced_pair` in
  `HexLLLMathlib/Basic.lean`, connecting the integer Lovász test the
  `lllLoop` body dispatches on at row `k`
  (`δ.den * (d[k+1] * d[k-1] + B²) ≥ δ.num * d[k]²`) to the rational
  Lovász predicate `isLLLReduced` checks for pair `(k-1, k)`
  (`δ * ‖b*[k-1]‖² ≤ ‖b*[k]‖² + μ² · ‖b*[k-1]‖²`).
- Proof route: `Valid.d_eq` translates `s.d` to `gramDet`,
  `lllState_ν_eq_coeffs` translates `B = ν[k][k-1]` to
  `(d[k] : Rat) · μ`, and `basis_normSq` translates each adjacent
  basis-norm to a ratio of consecutive Gram determinants. The
  algebraic content factors as
  `δ.den * (gd_k1 * gd_km1 + gd_k² * μ²) − δ.num * gd_k² =
     (δ.den · gd_k · gd_km1) · ((Nk + Nkm1·μ²) − δ·Nkm1)`,
  with the leading factor strictly positive by independence and
  `δ.den_pos`, so the two inequalities flip together.
